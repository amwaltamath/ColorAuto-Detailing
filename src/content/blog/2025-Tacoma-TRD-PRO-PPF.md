This 2025 Toyota Tacoma TRD PRO arrived at our Grand Junction shop ready for serious protection. The owner wanted to preserve that stunning deep gloss black paint while preparing for whatever adventures lie ahead. We delivered complete front-end PPF protection and professional ceramic coating.

## Front-End PPF Protection

We installed XPEL Ultimate+ PPF across the entire front end:

- **Full Front Bumper**: Complete coverage from wheel well to wheel well
- **Hood**: Full hood protection with seamless fit
- **Fenders**: Both front fenders wrapped and sealed
- **Mirrors**: Side mirror covers for complete protection
- **Headlights**: Precision-cut covers to protect lenses

## Why XPEL Ultimate+ for Off-Road?

The Tacoma TRD PRO is built for rugged terrain, but that doesn't mean you want rock chips ruining the paint. XPEL Ultimate+ provides:

- **Self-healing technology** - minor scratches disappear with heat
- **10-year warranty** - against yellowing, cracking, and peeling
- **Hydrophobic surface** - water beads off instantly
- **UV protection** - prevents oxidation and fading
- **Invisible protection** - maintains factory appearance

## Ceramic Coating Application

After PPF installation, we applied our premium ceramic coating to lock in that deep gloss:

- **Fusion Ceramic Coating** - professional-grade 9H hardness
- **Enhanced Gloss** - amplifies the deep black finish
- **Hydrophobic Properties** - water and contaminants bead off
- **Chemical Resistance** - protection against road salts and environmental damage
- **Easy Maintenance** - wash less frequently, stay cleaner longer

Our certified technicians spent 4 days completing this comprehensive protection package:
